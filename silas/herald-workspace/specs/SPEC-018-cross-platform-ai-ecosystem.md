## 1. PURPOSE

This specification defines Silas's methodology for building cross-platform content ecosystems that amplify a client's authority across every platform that AI systems crawl, index, and trust. While SPEC-017 focuses on direct LLM optimization, this spec addresses the upstream problem: creating the multi-platform content and presence that makes a business the "consensus answer" across the entire information ecosystem.

From the CATALYST methodology: "You're not just creating content; you're building a verifiable knowledge asset that exists across the entire information ecosystem that AI systems crawl and trust."

**Core principle:** "AI systems validate by consensus. The business that appears consistently across the most trusted platforms wins."

## 4. YOUTUBE OPTIMIZATION FOR LOCAL SEO

### 4.1 Why YouTube Matters

YouTube is the second-largest search engine and a primary content source for Google's AI systems. For local businesses, YouTube provides:
- Video results in Google search (including local queries)
- Rich transcript data that AI systems extract and cite
- YouTube-specific search queries ("how much does X cost in [city]")
- Multi-modal content signals (audio + visual + text)

### 4.2 Video Content Strategy for Local Businesses

**Content types ranked by impact:**

| Content Type | Example | Search Value | Production Effort |
|-------------|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Cost/Pricing guides | "AC Coil Replacement Cost Fort Lauderdale 2026" | Very High | Medium |
| How-to / Educational | "How to Choose a Plumber in Fort Lauderdale" | High | Medium |
| Project showcases | "Complete Kitchen Renovation ‚Äî Coral Ridge Home" | Medium | Low |
| FAQ compilations | "Top 10 Questions About Roof Repair" | High | Low |

| Emergency guides | "What to Do When Your AC Breaks at 2 AM" | Medium | Low |

### 4.3 Video Title Engineering

**Formula:** Primary Keyword + Location + Year + Price/Value Hook

```
Examples:
‚úÖ "AC Evaporator Coil Replacement Cost Fort Lauderdale 2026 | $1,800 - $4,300"
‚úÖ "Emergency Roof Repair Fort Lauderdale | What to Expect & What It Costs"

**Title character targets:**
- YouTube display: 60 characters visible in search
- Full title: up to 100 characters
- Primary keyword: within first 50 characters
- Location: always include city name

```

### 6.4 Reddit Presence (Critical for ChatGPT Training Data)

Reddit is one of the largest training data sources for ChatGPT and other LLMs.

```
Strategy (organic, not promotional):
1. Identify relevant local subreddits (r/[city], r/[industry])
2. Monitor for questions about client's service category
3. Provide genuinely helpful answers (not promotional)
4. Build account karma and credibility over time
5. When appropriate, mention client naturally:
   "When my AC broke, I used [Business Name] and they were there in 2 hours"
   
Caution:
- Never spam or overtly promote
- Reddit users are hostile to marketing
- Build genuine community presence first
- This is a long-term play (months to show results)
- Consider whether the effort justifies the return for each client
```

## 7. PARASITE SEO FOR AI VISIBILITY

### 7.1 What Is Parasite SEO?

Publishing content on high-authority third-party platforms to leverage their domain authority and crawl frequency. These platforms rank quickly and are frequently included in LLM training data.

### 7.2 Platform Priorities

| Platform | Domain Authority | LLM Training Source | Content Type |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Medium | Very High | Yes (ChatGPT, Perplexity) | Long-form articles |
| LinkedIn Articles | Very High | Yes (Copilot, ChatGPT) | Professional content |
| Quora | High | Yes (multiple LLMs) | Q&A responses |
| YouTube | Very High | Yes (all LLMs) | Video (covered in ¬ß4) |
| Google Docs (public) | Very High | Yes (Gemini) | Guides, checklists |
| GitHub Pages | High | Yes (ChatGPT) | Technical content |

### 7.3 Parasite Content Strategy

```
For each major client service:
1. Create definitive content on client website (canonical)
2. Republish adapted version on Medium
   - Different title, restructured content (avoid duplicate penalties)
   - Link back to client website as "original source"
3. Create LinkedIn Article version
   - Professional angle, shorter form
   - CTA to website for full details
4. Answer related Quora questions
   - Link to website for comprehensive answer
5. Monitor which parasite content ranks and generates traffic

Key rule: Website is always the canonical source. 
Parasite content always links back to it.
```

## 10. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC AI SIGNALS

### 10.1 The "Freshness Cascade"

When content is updated, the freshness signal cascades across platforms:

```
Day 1: Update core content on website
Day 1: GBP post announcing/reflecting the update
Day 2: YouTube video with updated information
Day 2: Social media posts referencing the change
Day 3: NotebookLM content refresh
Day 7: Medium article with updated perspective

Result: AI systems see "recent activity" across 6+ platforms within a week,
dramatically boosting recency signals for the next training data crawl.
```

### 10.2 Cross-Platform Citation Web

Every platform presence should reference other presences:

```
Website ‚Üí links to GBP, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Yelp, BBB
GBP ‚Üí links to website, YouTube channel, appointment URL
YouTube ‚Üí links to website, Google Maps, GBP in every description
Facebook ‚Üí links to website, YouTube, Instagram
LinkedIn ‚Üí links to website, YouTube, company projects
Medium ‚Üí links to website (canonical), YouTube
Schema markup ‚Üí sameAs array listing ALL platform URLs
```

This creates a web of cross-references that AI systems use to build entity confidence. When the same business appears connected across 10+ platforms with mutual references, entity confidence approaches certainty.
