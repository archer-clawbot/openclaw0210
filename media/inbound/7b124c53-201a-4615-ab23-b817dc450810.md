# LocalCatalyst.ai — Complete Topical Map & Content Architecture

## Document Purpose
This topical map defines every page on the LocalCatalyst.ai website, organized into hub (pillar) service pages and their supporting content clusters. The architecture is designed to maximize topical authority, internal linking density, and E-E-A-T signals for organic rankings in the local SEO agency vertical.

---

## Site Architecture Overview

```
HOME
├── HUB 1: Local SEO Services (Primary Pillar)
├── HUB 2: Google Business Profile Management
├── HUB 3: On-Page SEO
├── HUB 4: Technical SEO
├── HUB 5: SEO Content Strategy
├── HUB 6: Link Building Services
├── HUB 7: Citation Building & Management
├── HUB 8: Reputation Management
├── HUB 9: Website Design & Development
├── HUB 10: SEO Audits
├── INDUSTRY VERTICALS (Deep Niche Clusters w/ Supporting Content)
├── E-E-A-T TRUST ARCHITECTURE (About, Case Studies, etc.)
├── RESOURCE CENTER (Blog / Guides / Tools)
└── CONVERSION PAGES (Contact, Pricing, etc.)
```

## INDUSTRY VERTICAL CLUSTERS

Each industry vertical operates as a mini hub-and-spoke cluster — a pillar page targeting the primary "[industry] SEO" keyword, supported by 3–5 cluster pages that target long-tail, industry-specific queries. This builds deep topical authority in each niche, captures high-intent commercial traffic, and signals to Google that LocalCatalyst.ai has genuine expertise in serving these industries.

Each vertical pillar links back to 3–4 service hubs. Each cluster page links back to its vertical pillar AND to the most relevant service hub.

**URL Pattern:** `/industries/{industry}/` (pillar) → `/industries/{industry}/{topic}/` (cluster)

## INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY

### Linking Rules

1. **Every hub page links to ALL its cluster pages** in a structured "Related Services" or "Learn More" section
2. **Every cluster page links back to its parent hub** in the first 100 words
3. **Cross-hub linking**: Each hub page links to 2–3 other hub pages where contextually relevant
4. **Blog posts link to relevant service pages** as primary CTAs (not just other blog posts)
5. **Industry vertical pillars link to 3–4 service hubs** (e.g., Restaurant SEO → GBP Management, Reputation Management, Content Strategy)
6. **Industry cluster pages link to their vertical pillar AND the most relevant service hub**
7. **Cross-vertical linking** between related industries (e.g., Plumbing ↔ Home Services, Dental ↔ Medical)
8. **Case studies link to the service hub(s) that drove results** + the relevant industry vertical
9. **Homepage links to all 10 hub pages** in the services section + featured industry verticals
10. **Contextual anchor text**: Use keyword-rich anchors (not "click here" or "learn more")
11. **Breadcrumbs on every page**: Home > Hub > Page or Home > Industries > Vertical > Page
