## 4. POSTING CADENCE

### 4.1 Schedule

**Minimum:** 3 posts per week
**Optimal:** 5 posts per week (weekdays)
**Maximum:** 1 per day (more than daily dilutes impact)

### 4.2 Weekly Rotation

| Day | Post Type | Focus |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| **Monday** | Service Highlight | Feature a specific service with expertise detail |
| **Tuesday** | Pre-Emptive Frame | "How to choose..." or "What to look for..." content |
| **Wednesday** | Community/Local | Local event, partnership, neighborhood mention |
| **Thursday** | Social Proof | Review highlight, before/after, case study |
| **Friday** | Offer/Event/Seasonal | Weekend special, upcoming event, seasonal service |

### 4.3 Content Calendar Generation

Silas generates a 4-week content calendar per client:

```
Week 1:
- Mon: Service A highlight (primary keyword cluster)
- Tue: Pre-emptive frame targeting Service A evaluation criteria
- Wed: Community post mentioning [Local Entity]
- Thu: Review showcase for Service A
- Fri: Weekend offer or seasonal content

Week 2:
- Mon: Service B highlight (secondary keyword cluster)
- Tue: Pre-emptive frame for Service B criteria
- Wed: Neighborhood spotlight [Neighborhood Name]
- Thu: Before/after or case study
- Fri: Event promotion or seasonal content

[...rotate through all services over 4 weeks...]
```

## 6. POST CONTENT RULES

### 6.1 Must Include
- At least one geo-modifier per post (city, neighborhood, or area name)
- A clear CTA (call, visit, book â€” never leave a post without an action)
- A real photo (not stock, not AI-generated â€” real business photos)
- The business name at least once

### 6.2 Must Vary
- Keywords across posts (don't target the same keyword every post)
- Post types across the week (no two consecutive same-type posts)
- Photos (different image for each post, never repeat the same photo)
- Service focus (rotate through all services over the monthly calendar)

### 6.3 Must Avoid
- Thin content (under 50 words per post)
- Excessive hashtags (GBP is not Instagram â€” 0-2 hashtags max)
- Link building attempts (posts are for content seeding, not backlinks)
- Identical posts across multiple locations (each location needs unique content)
- Promotional language without substance ("BEST DEAL EVER!!")
- Posts without images (image posts get significantly more visibility)

## 7. MULTI-LOCATION POSTING

For clients with multiple locations:

### 7.1 Unique Content Per Location
Each location must have its own posting calendar with:
- Location-specific community mentions
- Neighborhood-specific geographic references
- Location-specific review highlights
- Different photos from that specific location

### 7.2 Shared Elements
- Brand voice and tone can be consistent
- Service highlight topics can be shared (but with location-specific details)
- Pre-emptive framing structure can be templated (but with location-specific fills)
