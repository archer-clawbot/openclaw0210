### 1. "Drafting Technique"
- Find influencers in your space (SEO, local marketing, agency owners)
- Comment on their posts **as soon as they post** with valuable insight
- People see your comment â†’ click your profile â†’ follow you
- Builds cold audience reach

**How to execute:**
- Turn on notifications for 5-10 influencers
- When they post, comment within first 30 minutes
- Add value in your comment (not just "great post!")

### 2. Lead Magnet Posts
Post format:
> "If you want to learn the 5 ways I rank local businesses #1, I created a free guide + checklist. **Comment YES below to get it.**"

**What happens:**
- When they comment, it spreads to *their* network
- Their connections see it â†’ some comment YES too

- Captures email â†’ enters your funnel
- High engagement = LinkedIn algorithm boosts reach

## ðŸŽ¯ Niche-Down Strategy

Two paths:

### Path A: Niche by Service (what you're doing now)
- "Local SEO" is already niched
- Easier to scale than "we do everything"
- Service delivery is streamlined

### Path B: Niche by Industry (more money, easier marketing)
- Pick one: electricians, restaurants, orthodontists, HVAC, plumbers
- All your case studies = that industry
- All your marketing = that industry language
- Referrals explode (orthodontist refers another orthodontist)
- Less competition
- Higher prices (specialist premium)

**Test this:** Build sister brand (chicagoelectricianmarketing.com) and test if industry-specific converts better than LocalCatalyst.ai generic positioning.

**Example from transcript:**
- Agency owner switched from "SEO for everyone" to "orthodontist SEO"
- Used orthodontist examples in every post
- Audience became familiar with that vertical
- Started getting orthodontist referrals
- Could charge more as a specialist

**Money vs Fulfillment:**
- Niche by industry = more money, easier sales, boring
- Niche by service = more variety, slightly harder sales, more fulfilling
