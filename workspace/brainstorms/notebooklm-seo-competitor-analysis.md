## ðŸ“‹ THE EXACT SEO WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Find Top Ranking Pages

**How to do it:**
1. Search your target keyword on Google
2. Copy URLs of top 10-20 results

**Example keyword:** "AI automation community"

**Pro tip:** Don't just do top 10. Do top 20 for deeper analysis.

---

### Step 2: Upload to NotebookLM

**How to do it:**
1. Go to NotebookLM
2. Create new notebook
3. Paste all URLs as sources
4. NotebookLM imports them instantly

**Optional:** Also upload your own content:
- Draft articles
- FAQs you've collected
- Research notes
- Internal docs

---

### Step 3: Give NotebookLM the Prompt

**The exact prompt:**
```
Analyze the content gaps, search intent, and keyword clusters for [YOUR KEYWORD].

Output a detailed SEO outline with:
- Title tag
- Meta description
- H2 headings
- H3 headings
- Structure for a 1500-word article
```

**Example (Julian's prompt):**
```
Create an SEO outline for "AI automation community."

Include:
- H1, H2, and H3 headings
- 30 LSI keywords
- 8 frequently asked questions
- Structure it for 1500 words
- Target search intent for people looking to join or learn about AI automation communities
```

**What NotebookLM does:**
- Compares your competitors
- Identifies what they're covering
- Finds the gaps you can fill
- Spots the keywords they're using
- Builds a rank-ready outline

**Time:** About 30 seconds

---

### Step 4: Review the Output

**What you get:**
- **Title tag** (SEO-optimized)
- **Meta description** (compelling, click-worthy)
- **Introduction angle** (how to hook readers)
- **H2 and H3 headings** (structured, logical flow)
- **LSI keywords** (30+ related terms)
  - Example: AI business tools, automation workflows, community support, AI learning resources
- **FAQ section** (8 questions with answers)
  - Example: "What is an AI automation community?"
  - Example: "How can AI automation help my business?"
  - Example: "What tools are used in AI automation?"

**Key insight:**
> "Because the outline was built from actual top-ranking content, it has a way better chance of ranking."
