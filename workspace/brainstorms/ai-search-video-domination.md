**1. Multiple Signals**

### Week 1: Keyword Research

**Goal:** Find questions people are asking in your niche

**Method 1: Google Autocomplete**
- Type your niche keyword into Google
- Look at auto-suggest results
- These are real questions people are asking

**Example:**
- Type: "Notebook LM"
- Auto-suggests: "Notebook LM update," "Notebook LM tutorial," "Notebook LM vs X"

**Method 2: ChatGPT Prompt**

```

Make them specific and long-tail.
```

**What you get:**
