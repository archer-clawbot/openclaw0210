## ðŸ’° STRATEGY #3: CUSTOMER MAGNET FRAMEWORK

### The Big Idea

**The problem most people have:**
> "Most people only create traffic content. They get traffic, but they don't get any customers."

**The solution:** Balance three types of content

---

### The 3 Types of SEO Content

#### 1. Traffic Content
**Purpose:** Get eyeballs

**Examples:**
- "What is [topic]"
- Listicles ("10 best...")
- How-to guides (general)
- Definition posts

**Characteristics:**
- High volume keywords
- Informational intent
- Attracts browsers, not buyers
- Top of funnel

**Julian's take:**
> "Most people only create option one, which is traffic content. They get traffic, but they don't get any customers."

---

#### 2. Trust Content
**Purpose:** Build authority

**Examples:**
- Case studies
- Behind-the-scenes
- Strategy breakdowns
- Industry insights
- "How I did X" posts

**Characteristics:**
- Shows expertise
- Builds credibility
- Positions you as authority
- Middle of funnel

---

#### 3. Transaction Content
**Purpose:** Convert browsers into buyers

**Examples:**
- "[Product A] vs [Product B]"
- "Best [tool] for [specific use case]"
- "[Service] reviews"
- "Should you use [solution]?"

**Characteristics:**
- High buyer intent
- Comparison terms
- Decision-making queries
- Bottom of funnel

**Julian's note:**
> "Transaction content captures people who are ready to take action. This is the content that converts in days, not months."

---

### Transaction Content Prompt (Claude)

**The prompt:**
```
Create a comparison article: [AI Profit Boardroom vs Learning Alone]

TARGET AUDIENCE: [Your ideal customer]

STRUCTURE:
- Hook (why this matters)
- What most people try (and why it fails)
- The community/product approach
- Direct comparison table
- Real member results
- Who this is for (and who it's not for)

TONE:
- Genuinely helpful, not salesy
- Reader should feel informed, not sold to
- Use stories and examples
- Include real testimonials

Make it 1,500 words.
```

**What this creates:**
- Bottom-of-funnel content
- High conversion rate
- Captures people at decision moment
- Ranks AND converts

---

### Why This Framework Works

**The shift:**
> "Like I used to celebrate every traffic spike like 'Ooo we hit 10,000 visitors per month.' But my business wasn't growing because those visitors weren't buyers. They were reading like 'what is SEO' content which is interesting but they weren't ready to take action. When I shifted to the customer magnet content method, traffic actually dropped but leads went up because I was attracting the right people."

**Key insight:**
> "Traffic is a vanity metric. Customers are the real score."

---

### Low Volume Buyer Keywords > High Volume Browser Keywords

**Example comparison:**

**Browser keyword:**
- "What is email marketing"
- Volume: 50,000 searches/month
- Buyer intent: LOW
- Conversion rate: 0.1%

**Buyer keyword:**
- "Best email marketing tool for coaches"
- Volume: 500 searches/month
- Buyer intent: HIGH
- Conversion rate: 5-10%

**Question:**
> "Would you rather have 10,000 people who kind of sort of might be interested or 100 people who are ready to buy? I know which one I'd choose."

**Julian's strategy:** Target buyer keywords, not volume keywords
