## Database Scraping Strategy

### **Goal:** Build 10,000+ lead database for cold email + retargeting

### **Tools for Scraping**

**1. Apollo.io ($49-99/month)**
- Best for: B2B contact data (business owners, marketing managers)
- Filters: Industry, location, company size, job title, revenue
- Export: CSV with name, email, phone, company, website
- Limit: 10,000 contacts/month on Growth plan

**2. Hunter.io ($49-149/month)**
- Best for: Finding email addresses for specific domains
- Use case: You have a list of businesses, need their emails
- Chrome extension: Find emails while browsing websites

**3. Instant Data Scraper (Chrome Extension, Free)**
- Best for: Scraping Google Maps results
- Use case: Search "restaurants in Houston" â†’ scrape business names, addresses, phones, websites
- Export: CSV
- Limitation: Manual (you have to run searches yourself)

**4. Outscraper ($29/month)**
- Best for: Bulk Google Maps scraping
- Use case: "plumbers in texas" â†’ scrapes all GBP listings (name, address, phone, website, reviews, hours)
- Export: CSV
- Limit: 20,000 results/month

**5. Phantombuster ($59/month)**
- Best for: LinkedIn scraping
- Use case: Extract LinkedIn profiles who match your ICP (industry, job title, location)
- Export: CSV with name, email (if public), LinkedIn URL, company
