**Goal:** Understand their problem, qualify them

**Questions:**
1. "Tell me about your business. How do you currently get customers?"

3. "Have you tried SEO before? What happened?"
4. "If I could show you how to get 2-3x more customers from Google, would that be worth $2k/month?"
5. "What's your timeline? Are you ready to start in the next 30 days?"

**Qualify:**
- âœ… Business is profitable (>$500k/year revenue)

### Step 2: Audit Presentation (30 min)
**Goal:** Show them what's broken, how you'll fix it

**Agenda:**
1. Show current rankings (0-3 on Google for target keywords)
2. Show competitor rankings (#1-3)
3. Explain the gap (technical issues, thin content, no GBP optimization, no citations)
4. Present 90-day plan (what you'll do each month)
5. Show case study (social proof)
6. Present pricing + next steps
