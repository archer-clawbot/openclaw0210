## PART 4: OPTIONAL PILLAR EXPANSION

### Should a Fitness/Crossfit Sandbags Pillar Be Added?

**The case for it:** There's a meaningful fitness-sandbag keyword cluster:
- "sandbag workout" — 12,000–20,000/mo
- "sandbag training" — 8,000–15,000/mo  
- "crossfit sandbag" — 3,000–6,000/mo
- "sandbag exercises" — 6,000–10,000/mo

**The case against:** This audience is completely separate from the flood/emergency/commercial audience. Fitness sandbags are a different product sold in different channels. Adding a fitness pillar risks confusing the site's topical identity and splitting link equity between unrelated verticals. palmetto-industries.com does not cover fitness. The monetization angle (delivery service, bulk sandbag filling) has no connection to fitness users.

**Recommendation:** **Do not add Fitness pillar at launch.** If the operator has a separate lead gen opportunity in fitness (gym equipment rental, CrossFit gym supply), that warrants a separate site or subdomain. Do not dilute the flood/emergency topical authority that will be the site's ranking engine.
