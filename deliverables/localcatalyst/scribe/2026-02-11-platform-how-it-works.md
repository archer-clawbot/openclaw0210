## How the Order Flow Works

### 1. Browse Products

Every SEO deliverable we offer has a dedicated product page with transparent pricing, clear scope, and a defined turnaround time. No custom quotes. No "schedule a call to learn more." You see the price, you see what you get, you decide.

Products range from one-time deliverables like [citation building](/services/citation-building/) and [website builds](/services/website-build/) to strategic assets like SEO audits, topical maps, and content packages.

### 2. Enter Your Details

Each product has a structured intake form. You provide the information our system needs — your business name, address, website, target keywords, service areas, or whatever the specific deliverable requires.

No lengthy onboarding calls. No back-and-forth email chains. You fill out the form, and our system has everything it needs to start work.

### 3. AI Agents Execute

This is where LocalCatalyst separates from everything else on the market.

Your order is assigned to a team of [specialized AI agents](/platform/technology/) — not a single generalist, but multiple agents each handling the discipline they were built for. An audit runs through our research agent, strategy agent, and technical agent simultaneously. A content order goes through our keyword researcher, content strategist, and content writer in sequence.

Every deliverable follows our [CATALYST methodology](/platform/methodology/), the same framework we used on 20+ real client campaigns before opening this platform to the public.

### 4. Download Your Deliverables

Turnaround times vary by product — most deliverables are ready within hours, some within a few business days. You receive a notification when your order is complete, download the finished work, and implement it.

Every deliverable comes in a format you can use immediately: structured documents, spreadsheets, PDF reports, or direct implementation depending on the product.

## Why This Beats Hiring an Agency

Agencies are slow, expensive, and opaque. The typical local SEO agency charges $1,500-$5,000 per month on a 6-12 month contract. You wait weeks for deliverables. You never know exactly what work is being done or when it will be finished.

LocalCatalyst eliminates every one of those friction points:

- **Speed.** Hours, not weeks. Our AI agents work around the clock.
- **Cost.** Pay per deliverable, not per month. No retainers, no contracts.
- **Transparency.** Every product has a fixed price and defined scope before you buy.
- **Quality.** 18 specialized agents outperform one generalist account manager every time.

If you need full-service managed SEO, we offer that too — but the pricing page exists because we believe you should know what you are paying for before you commit.

SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz, BrightLocal — they are all excellent tools. But they are tools. They give you dashboards and data and expect you to do the work yourself.

LocalCatalyst is not a tool. You do not log in to run reports. You do not spend hours interpreting data or writing content or submitting citations.
