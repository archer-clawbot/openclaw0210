## Google Partner

LocalCatalyst is a certified Google Partner, which means we have met Google's requirements for demonstrating expertise in Google Ads and Google's advertising products. Google Partner status requires:

- Passing Google Ads product certification exams
- Meeting performance benchmarks for managed ad spend and client results
- Maintaining ongoing compliance with Google's best practices

While our core service is organic local SEO, Google Partner status reflects our team's deep understanding of the full Google ecosystem --- including how paid and organic search interact in local markets. This knowledge allows us to advise clients on holistic search strategies and identify opportunities where organic and paid efforts can complement each other.

## Continuing Education Commitment

The search industry does not stand still, and neither do we. Our team maintains a structured continuing education program that includes:

- **Algorithm update research:** When Google releases a core update or local-specific update, our team analyzes ranking volatility across our client portfolio, identifies patterns, and adjusts strategies proactively --- not reactively.
- **Industry conference attendance:** Our team regularly attends and participates in events such as MozCon, BrightonSEO, LocalU, and the Local Search Summit to stay connected with the latest research, case studies, and practitioner insights.
- **Internal training sessions:** We hold bi-weekly team training sessions where team members present research findings, test results, and new techniques. Knowledge sharing is built into our operating rhythm.
- **Publication and community involvement:** Our team members contribute articles, case studies, and analyses to industry publications, maintaining active engagement with the broader SEO community.

## Tool Proficiency

Our team is proficient in the full stack of industry-standard SEO tools, ensuring we can extract maximum insight from every data source:

- **Ahrefs** — Backlink analysis, content gap research, keyword exploration, site auditing
- **SEMrush** — Position tracking, competitive analysis, technical auditing, keyword research
- **BrightLocal** — Local rank tracking, citation auditing, GBP management, review monitoring
- **Screaming Frog** — Technical crawl analysis, site architecture mapping, redirect chain identification
- **Google Search Console** — Index coverage monitoring, search performance analysis, Core Web Vitals tracking
- **RankMath** — On-page optimization, schema markup implementation, WordPress SEO configuration
- **Google Business Profile** — Profile optimization, post scheduling, review management, insights analysis
- **Geo-grid ranking tools** — Hyperlocal rank tracking across geographic coordinates in client service areas

Tool proficiency is not about having access to software. It's about knowing which tool to use for which question, how to interpret its output correctly, and how to translate that data into actionable strategy.
