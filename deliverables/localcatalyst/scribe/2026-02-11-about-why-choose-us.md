## Real Metrics, Not Vanity Reports

If your current agency sends you a monthly report that shows "average position" improving and calls it a day, you're not getting the full picture. Average position is a vanity metric. It tells you almost nothing about what's actually happening in your local market.

LocalCatalyst tracks the metrics that actually predict revenue:

- **Geo-Grid Rankings:** We don't just check if you rank #3 for "plumber near me." We check where you rank across a grid of geographic points in your service area, because local rankings change block by block.
- **Share of Local Voice (SoLV):** What percentage of total local search visibility do you own versus your competitors? This is the metric that tells you whether you're winning or losing the market.
- **Weighted Visibility Score (WVS):** Not all keywords are created equal. WVS weights your ranking positions by search volume, commercial intent, and conversion probability to give you a single score that correlates with actual business outcomes.
- **Competitive Displacement:** We don't just track your movement --- we track who you're displacing and who's gaining on you. Local SEO is a zero-sum game. Someone has to lose for you to win.

Every client gets dashboard access to these metrics in real time. No waiting for a monthly PDF to find out what's happening with your campaign.

## The CATALYST Methodology

Most local SEO agencies operate on instinct. They do a little keyword research, optimize your GBP, write some blog posts, and hope for the best. When something works, they don't always know why. When something doesn't, they don't have a framework to diagnose the problem.

LocalCatalyst's CATALYST Methodology is an engineered, repeatable process that eliminates guesswork:

1. **Audit:** Comprehensive technical, content, competitive, and local visibility audit. We identify every factor that's helping or hurting your rankings, scored and prioritized by impact.
2. **Prioritize:** Not all fixes are equal. We build a prioritized roadmap based on effort-to-impact ratio, focusing first on the changes that will move the needle fastest.
3. **Execute:** Systematic implementation with documented deliverables, clear timelines, and weekly progress tracking. No vague promises --- specific actions with measurable outcomes.
4. **Expand:** Once foundational wins are secured, we expand into new keywords, new service areas, new content opportunities, and competitive displacement strategies that compound your growth over time.

CATALYST isn't a marketing term. It's the operational framework that guides every decision we make on every client campaign.
