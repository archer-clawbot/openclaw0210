### H2: What Is Google Business Profile Optimization?

Your **Google Business Profile** (formerly Google My Business or GMB) is the profile that appears when someone searches for your business or services on Google Maps or Google Search. It's the digital storefront that can make or break your local visibility.

**Your GBP includes:**
- Business name, address, phone (NAP)
- Business category and attributes
- Business description (750 characters)
- Services list with detailed descriptions
- Products tab for offerings or packages
- Photos and videos
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Q&A section
- Posts (updates, offers, events)
- Website link and booking options

### H2: Google Business Profile Optimization FAQs

**Q: What's the difference between GMB, GBP, and Google My Business?**  
A: They're the same thing. Google rebranded "Google My Business" (GMB) to "Google Business Profile" (GBP) in 2021. The platform and functionality are the same. We use GBP, but many people still call it GMB.

**Q: How long does GBP optimization take to see results?**  
A: Quick wins (description, services, Q&A optimization) can show ranking movement within 2-4 weeks. Sustained Map Pack domination with reviews and consistent posting typically takes 8-12 weeks for full impact.

**Q: Can I optimize my GBP myself?**  
A: You can handle basics (claim profile, add photos, respond to reviews), but advanced tactics like entity co-citation engineering, Q&A seeding, and AI Overview optimization require specialized expertise and consistent time investment.

**Q: Do I need a physical location to have a GBP?**  
A: No. Service-area businesses (SABs) like plumbers, landscapers, and mobile services can have a GBP without displaying a physical address. You simply hide your address and show your service area coverage.

**Q: What if I have multiple locations?**  
A: Each location gets its own GBP. We optimize each individually with location-specific content while maintaining brand consistency. We offer multi-location packages with volume pricing.

**Q: How many posts should I publish per week?**  
A: Minimum 3x/week to signal an active profile to Google. We recommend 3-5x/week for maximum impact. Consistency matters more than frequency â€” better to do 3/week consistently than 10 one week and zero the next.

**Q: Can negative reviews hurt my rankings?**  
A: Yes, but overall rating, review volume, and velocity matter more than a few negative reviews. A 4.5 rating with 100 reviews will outrank a 5.0 rating with 8 reviews. We have a negative review mitigation strategy to minimize damage and show responsiveness.

**Q: What if my competitor has more reviews than me?**  
A: Review count matters, but it's not the only factor. We optimize all other ranking signals (services, description, Q&A, posts, categories) while building your review count. Often we can help you outrank competitors with 2-3x more reviews through superior optimization.
