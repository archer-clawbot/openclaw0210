#### We Track Real Visibility, Not Vanity Metrics

We don't report meaningless metrics like "average ranking position" or "domain authority."

**We track what matters:**

**Geo-Grid Rankings:**  
Heat maps showing exactly where you rank across dozens of points in your service area. Your ranking changes based on searcher location — we track all of it.

**SoLV (Share of Local Voice):**  
Your percentage of total Map Pack appearances compared to competitors. This shows your true market share of visibility.

**WVS (Weighted Visibility Score):**  
Position-weighted ranking quality. #1 rankings matter more than #10 rankings — WVS accounts for that.

When you overtake a competitor in the Map Pack, we track it. You'll know exactly when you win and who you beat.

### H2: Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring an SEO Agency

**Q: How long does SEO take to show results?**  
A: Quick wins (like GBP optimization or title tag fixes) can show movement in 2-4 weeks. Comprehensive SEO campaigns typically show significant results within 8-12 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline based on your market competitiveness.

**Q: Can you guarantee #1 rankings?**  
A: No. Anyone who guarantees #1 rankings is lying or using black-hat tactics that will get you penalized. We guarantee transparent methodology, consistent execution, and measurable progress. Rankings follow when you do SEO right.

**Q: What's the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?**  
A: Local SEO focuses on Map Pack rankings and location-based searches ("near me" queries). Regular SEO focuses on organic rankings in standard search results. Local businesses need both, but local SEO drives more immediate business results.

**Q: Do I need an SEO agency or can I do it myself?**  
A: You can do basic SEO yourself (claim your GBP, optimize titles, write content). But competitive markets require technical expertise, strategic execution, and consistent effort that most business owners don't have time for. Agencies deliver ROI by getting results faster and better than DIY.

**Q: How do I know if an SEO agency is legit?**  
A: Look for:
- Transparent methodology (they explain what they do)
- Realistic expectations (no overnight results promises)
- Case studies with real data
- Client testimonials from businesses like yours
- Technical expertise (can they explain Core Web Vitals, schema, crawlability?)

**Q: What's the ROI of hiring an SEO agency?**  
A: It depends on your business and market. A local service business ranking #1 for high-intent keywords can generate 10-50+ leads per month worth thousands in revenue. If SEO generates $10K/month in new business and costs $2K/month, that's 5x ROI.
