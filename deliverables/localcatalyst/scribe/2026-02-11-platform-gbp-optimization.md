## What We Optimize

### Business Description
Your GBP description is 750 characters of prime real estate. Most businesses waste it with generic copy like "We've been serving the community for 20 years." We rewrite it to include your primary keywords, service area, key differentiators, and a clear call to action -- all within Google's guidelines.

### Categories
Google allows one primary category and up to nine secondary categories. The right combination directly affects which searches your profile appears for. We audit your current categories against competitor profiles and search demand, then recommend the optimal set.

### Services and Products
The services section is an underused ranking signal. We build out complete service listings with keyword-optimized names and descriptions. Each entry becomes another data point Google uses to match your profile with relevant searches.

### Q&A Section
Google's Q&A section is open to anyone -- including your competitors. We pre-seed 10-15 questions and answers that cover your most common customer inquiries, target relevant keywords, and prevent misinformation from showing up on your profile.

### Posting Strategy
GBP posts signal to Google that your business is active and engaged. A consistent posting schedule -- even once or twice a week -- correlates with stronger map pack presence. We build a 30-day calendar with topics tied to your services and seasonal demand.

### Attributes and Details
Business hours, holiday hours, payment methods, accessibility features, amenities, health and safety practices -- every attribute Google offers for your business type, filled in completely. Completeness is a ranking signal. Most competitors skip these.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you need my Google account login?**
No. You add our optimization team as a Manager on your Google Business Profile. We never need your personal Google credentials. You can remove access after the optimization is complete.

**How do I implement the changes?**
For the standard tier, we deliver a document with every change specified -- copy it into your GBP field by field. It takes about 30 minutes. For the posts tier, the posts are delivered ready to copy-paste into the GBP posting interface, one at a time on the dates specified in the calendar.

**Will this guarantee I rank in the map pack?**
No one can guarantee map pack rankings. Google's local algorithm considers proximity, relevance, and prominence. GBP optimization directly improves the relevance and prominence signals. Combined with strong reviews and a well-optimized website, it significantly increases your probability of map pack placement.

**What if my GBP has been suspended?**
We don't handle suspension recovery as part of this product. If your profile is currently suspended, resolve that first (Google has a reinstatement process), then order optimization once your profile is active again.

**Can I order this for multiple locations?**
Yes. Multi-location businesses get a 15% discount on orders of 5+ locations. Each location receives its own fully customized optimization. [Browse Our Services](/services/) for multi-location pricing details.
