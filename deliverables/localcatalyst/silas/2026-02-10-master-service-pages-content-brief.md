## 7. FAQ / GROUNDING BOX STRATEGY (SPEC-007)

### What Are Grounding Boxes?

**Grounding boxes** are FAQ-style content blocks engineered to:
1. Target "People Also Ask" SERP features
2. Provide context for AI Overviews (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE)
3. Answer objections/common questions inline
4. Improve content structure and scannability

### Required FAQ Sections per Service Page

**Every service page should have an FAQ section with 5-8 questions minimum.**

#### Local SEO Services — Sample FAQs:

1. **How long does local SEO take to show results?**  
   Answer: Typical timeline is 60-90 days for initial Map Pack visibility, 6 months for market dominance. We guarantee measurable improvements within 90 days.

2. **Do I need a physical address for local SEO?**  
   Answer: Yes, for most local businesses. If you're a service area business (SAB), you can hide your address but still need a registered location for verification.

3. **What's the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?**  
   Answer: Local SEO targets geo-specific queries ("plumber near me") and optimizes for Map Pack visibility. Regular SEO targets national keywords without location modifiers.

4. **How much does local SEO cost?**  
   Answer: Our local SEO packages start at $2,500/month. Pricing depends on market competitiveness, service area size, and number of target keywords.

5. **Can I do local SEO myself?**  
   Answer: Yes, but it's time-intensive. GBP optimization, citation building, review management, and technical SEO require specialized knowledge and 10-20 hours/week minimum.

#### Technical SEO Services — Sample FAQs:

1. **What is a technical SEO audit?**  
   Answer: A comprehensive analysis of your site's crawlability, indexation, site speed, schema markup, mobile responsiveness, and Core Web Vitals performance.

2. **How much does a technical SEO audit cost?**  
   Answer: Our standalone technical SEO audit is $800. This includes a detailed report with prioritized recommendations and implementation timeline.

3. **What are Core Web Vitals?**  
   Answer: Core Web Vitals are Google's page experience metrics: LCP (loading speed), INP (interactivity), and CLS (visual stability). Passing CWV is a ranking factor.

4. **Do I need technical SEO if my site is new?**  
   Answer: Absolutely. New sites often have critical indexation issues, missing schema, or slow load times that prevent Google from ranking pages.

#### On-Page SEO Services — Sample FAQs:

1. **What is on-page SEO?**  
   Answer: On-page SEO is optimizing individual web pages (title tags, meta descriptions, content, internal links, images) to rank higher and drive traffic.

2. **How is on-page SEO different from content writing?**  
   Answer: Content writing creates new content. On-page SEO optimizes existing (or new) content for search engines AND user experience.

3. **Can on-page SEO improve my rankings quickly?**  
   Answer: Yes. On-page fixes (title tag optimization, schema markup, internal linking) can show results in 2-4 weeks — faster than off-page SEO.

#### GBP Optimization — Sample FAQs:

1. **What is Google Business Profile optimization?**  
   Answer: GBP optimization is engineering every element of your Google Business Profile (description, services, Q&A, posts, photos) for Map Pack rankings and conversions.

2. **How long does GBP optimization take?**  
   Answer: Initial optimization takes 1-2 weeks. Ongoing optimization (weekly posts, Q&A monitoring, review responses) is continuous.

3. **Do I need GBP if I have a website?**  
   Answer: Yes. Your GBP is the #1 ranking factor for appearing in the Map Pack. 75% of local searchers never scroll past the Map Pack to see organic results.

## 11. POST-DEPLOYMENT OPTIMIZATION

### Week 1-2: Initial Performance Tracking

**Metrics to Monitor:**
- Impressions in Google Search Console (are pages being indexed?)
- Average position for target keywords
- Click-through rate (CTR) from search results
- Bounce rate (high bounce = content mismatch or poor UX)
- Time on page (low time = content not engaging)
- Conversion rate (form submissions, consultation bookings)

**Actions:**
- If CTR < 2%: Rewrite meta titles/descriptions for better SERP appeal
- If bounce rate > 60%: Improve page speed, add internal links, enhance visuals
- If time on page < 2 min: Content may be too thin or poorly structured
- If conversion rate < 3%: Test different CTA placements, simplify forms

### Month 1-3: Content Refinement

**Based on Search Console data:**
1. **Identify underperforming keywords** (high impressions, low clicks)
   - Optimize title/meta for those queries
   - Add dedicated H2 sections targeting those keywords
2. **Find long-tail opportunities** (queries with impressions but not targeted)
