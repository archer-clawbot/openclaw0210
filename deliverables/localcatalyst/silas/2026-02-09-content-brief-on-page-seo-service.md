### Section 1: What Is On-Page SEO? (Educational)
**H2:** What Is On-Page SEO and Why It Matters

**Content:**

**On-page SEO** (also called on-page optimization or on-site SEO) is the practice of optimizing individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic. It includes:

**Content Optimization:**
- Keyword research and targeting
- Content quality and depth
- Semantic keyword usage
- Content freshness and updates

**HTML Optimization:**
- Title tags
- Meta descriptions
- Header tags (H1, H2, H3)
- Image alt text
- URL structure

**User Experience Optimization:**
- Internal linking
- Content formatting (readability, scannability)
- Calls to action (CTAs)
- Multimedia (images, videos)

**Why It Matters:**  
On-page SEO is what tells Google **what your page is about**. Without it, even the best content won't rank for the keywords that matter.

### Section 7: FAQ
**H2:** On-Page SEO Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the difference between on-page SEO and content marketing?**  
A: On-page SEO optimizes existing content (titles, structure, keywords). Content marketing creates new content (blog posts, guides, videos). We do both.

**Q: How long does it take to see results from on-page SEO?**  
A: Quick wins (title tag optimization) can show ranking improvements within 2-4 weeks. Comprehensive content optimization typically shows full impact within 8-12 weeks.

**Q: Can you optimize pages I've already written?**  
A: Yes. We can audit and optimize existing content without rewriting from scratch (unless it's thin or low-quality).

**Q: Do I need on-page SEO if I have a small site?**  
A: Yes. Even a 5-page site needs optimized title tags, meta descriptions, and strategic keyword targeting.

**Q: Will on-page SEO improve my conversion rate?**  
A: Yes. We optimize for conversions (CTA placement, readability, benefit-driven copy), not just rankings.
