## Key Optimizations Applied

### 1. **Added Compelling CTAs**
- Every page now includes clear calls-to-action
- CTA types: "Free estimate," "Call now," "Free quote"
- CTAs are action-oriented and specific

**Example:**
- **Before:** "Residential Electrician Chicago | Home Electrical Services | Chicago's Electrician"
- **After:** "Residential Electrician Chicago | Same-Day Repairs & Upgrades"
- **Meta:** "Need a residential electrician in Chicago? Same-day repairs, lighting, panel upgrades. Licensed, insured. Free estimate. Call now for emergency service!"

### 2. **Incorporated Urgency Signals**
- "24/7" (4 pages)
- "Same-day" (8 pages)
- Creates emotional urgency without being pushy

### 3. **Added Trust Credentials**
- "Licensed & insured" appears in most pages
- "25+ years experience" for location pages
- "Family-owned" where relevant (Winnetka)

### 4. **Optimized for Click-Through**
- Removed redundancy (eliminated "Chicago's Electrician" from most pages)
- Titles reduced from average 70 chars → 62 chars (more readable in SERPs)
- Meta descriptions tightened to 155-159 chars (optimal display on mobile)

### 5. **Improved Title Tag Diversity**
- Before: Many titles started with "[Service Type] [City]"
- After: Mixed structures with varied CTAs to stand out in SERPs
  - Homepage: "24/7 Chicago Electrician | Same-Day Service | Free Estimate"
  - Residential: "Residential Electrician Chicago | Same-Day Repairs & Upgrades"
  - Contact: "Free Electrical Estimate Chicago | 24/7 Emergency Service"
