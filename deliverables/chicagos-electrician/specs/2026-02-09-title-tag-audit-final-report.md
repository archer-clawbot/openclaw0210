## Executive Summary

This audit analyzed the top 10 high-traffic pages on chicagoselectrician.com for title tag and meta description optimization. The analysis identified significant gaps in CTAs, urgency signals, and click-through optimization. **All recommended changes have been executed and are currently live on the site.**

**Key Findings:**
- 2/10 pages missing meta descriptions entirely
- All pages lack compelling CTAs (zero urgency drivers)
- Redundant language reducing relevance signals
- Average title length suboptimal for SERP display
- Untapped trust credential opportunities

**Quick Win Potential:** Title/meta optimization typically yields 15-30% CTR improvement and indexes within 48-72 hours.
